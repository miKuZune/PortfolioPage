    A little about me!

    Hi there! My name's Max and I'm a programmer mainly focused on creating Artificial Intelligence for games to help immerse and challenge players. I also do a lot of Gameplay programming. Using these skills I aim to create the most engaging experiences possible.

    I'm currently a student in my final year at Falmouth University studying Computing for Games(BSC). I mainly work with Unity but also have experience with Unreal. I've worked with a variety of languages such as C#, C++, Java, Javascript, SQL, HTML and CSS.

    I'm a keen student and a hard-worker, constantly looking to learn new techniques and expand my technical knowledge through practical and theoretical learning.

Game Jams

Penguin Planet - GlobalGameJam 2019

Penguin Planet is a light-hearted resource management game where you have to send your rather well-dressed penguins off to collect resources needed for the colony.

For this project I was the only programmer on a team of 8 and was responsible for the programming as well as working with the rest of the team to rapidly implement their work.

Link to the page below.

Mecha Melee: Rome Edition - GlobalGameJam 2019

MechaMelee is a Sci-Fi gladiatorial where you fight as one of many robots trying to survive the onslaught of the arena. Your main ability is the power to transfer your AI/consciousness into the other robots, allowing you to regain your health and continue the battle.

For this project the team's skill sets not very diverse as the team was made up of 4 programmers and 3 artists. As such we all had to take on roles that were outside out comfort zones.

In my case I took on a leadership and design role for the project, in addition to programming the AI. I was responsible for giving each member of the team their tasks, based on what skills they possessed outside of the specialties. I also took on the design role, creating the level for the game to be played in and ensuring that all members of the team had a shared vision of the game. For this game I also programmed the AI, which presented additional challenges because of the main mechanic of being able to take control of the enemies.

Link to the GlobalGameJam & GameJolt pages below if you want to try it out.

Ludum Dare 2017 - Toy Hero

Toy Hero is a game where you play as a toy robot who has to run around a child's bedroom, hiding as many other toys as possible before mum tidies them away, never to be seen again. It's a round based game with a time limit that gives you score for however many toys you manage to hide. The more toys you save in one round the more there will be to save in the next.

This was my first game jam and I created the game on my own. Through this project I learned a lot about areas of game development outside of programming, which has helped me a lot when working with others to implement their work into the project.

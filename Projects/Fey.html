Fey is a 2.5D metroidvania game featuring combat, exploration & puzzle solving.

My main role for this project was to create the enemies the player would encounter as they traverse the world. When creating the enemies AI I used state machines to dictate an enemies behavior. This allowed for the AIâ€™s to quickly change their behaviors based on certain triggers, such as the player getting too close or taking damage. This also allowed for some behaviors to be shared between multiple enemies where applicable, helping to save programming time, reduce redundant code & lowering the amount of time needed for testing.

While my main role was to create the enemies I was also responsible for many other parts of the project. Creating a save system, working on parts of the puzzle systems & building tools for artists & designers that helped them to implement their work. I was also responsible for our version control, helping other less technical members of the team to upload their work and resolving conflicts as they arose.

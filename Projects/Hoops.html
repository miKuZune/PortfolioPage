A web based game made as an online promotion for the 2021 Super Bowl. The game was a timer based round in which the player would have to throw as many basketballs into the hoop as possible. When they player scored multiple hoops in a row they would get a multiplier to their score which would then enable them to get more points for scoring many hoops in a row as well as changing the look of the game environment. During the last part of the timer the game would enter “golden time”, this also changed the way the environment looked and increased the amount of score the player would receive, however it also made it more difficult for the player by moving the hoop further away.

For this project, I also worked on other aspects than just the game. As this project was for Death Row Records I created a music player that would allow players to play some of the DRR music while they played the game. I also created an online leaderboard using PHP & an SQL database.
